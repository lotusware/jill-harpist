THE HARP PERFORMANCES BOOKING

Music to soothe the heart and soul

I believe that the role of the Harp should lend an air of “simple elegance”. Performing in a tent on the beach during sunset to the grandeur of a Cathedral, its music is well suited to a gathering. From a small and imitate atmosphere to a large ballroom setting the Harp has a tangible effect as it Soothes the Heart and Soul to all who hear.
